$$
\Phi=(\phi(x_1),\phi(x_2),\cdots,\phi(x_N))^T=
\begin{pmatrix}
    1 & \phi_1(x_1) & \phi_2(x_1) & \cdots & \phi_{M-1}(x_1) \\
    & & \vdots & & \\
    & & \phi_(x_N) & &
\end{pmatrix}
$$

$$
